Overview
My project will simulate a nuclear power plant over the next 36 hours and its components, designed to measure
and manage the cores over time, and hopefully prevent or predict any meltdowns!

Implementation
I will use a map for each of the different systems which help manage the nuclear power plant.
e.g core, cooling, waste as the keys. Under each of them there will be 3 lists for the data.
e.g active elements, electricity draws, temperatures. The map's value will be an array of
these 3 list data points.

Simulated Events
The simulation will have 3 possible events. Normal electricity and temperature fluctuation,
Unexpected temperature spikes, and activation or deactivation of elements. At any given time
period, temperature and electricity may fluctuate, temperature may get dangerously hot,
or some elements like cooling rods may be activated/deactivated.
